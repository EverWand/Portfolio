Battle Procrastination

Welcome to SideQuest


SideQuest is a tool made for users to gain a better habit of tracking their tasks and being more precise with how long they believe they will be able to complete tasks to help plan their workflow. The user will create their tasks and set up a target timeframe for how long it takes to complete it. Precision is rewarded using familiar RPG mechanics such as attacking an enemy, with damage being based on the difficulty/the length of the task, how close the completion time was to the target time, and if the task was urgent.
